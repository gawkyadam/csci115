The power supply is the powerhouse of the computer pumping electricity into every component in the system. It does this by taking the active current that is in the outlet in your walls and reduces the voltage using a power transformer, usually to the amount of voltage required by the load on the computer. It is important that it has the transformer so that it doesn't give too much voltage and kills the component its sending power to.
