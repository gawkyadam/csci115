There are mainly 2 types of storage devices used in PCs, Solid State Drives(SSD), and Hard Disk Drive(HDD), an SSD works by storing data permanently inside an intergrated circuit using flash memory. Which means that unlike an HDD data is written, transferred and erased digitally. HDD's on the other hand contains a spinning platter with a very thin magnetic coating that has a "head" moving over the platter, writing tiny 0's and 1's as tiny areas of magnetic North or South on the platter. To read the data back, the head goes to the same spot, notices the North and South spots flying by, and so deduces the stored 0's and 1's.
